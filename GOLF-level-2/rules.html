DM Cup 2020

Tournament Rules

Points system:

4 Match Types (major, mid, tour, and preseason)

Preseason - 1 point for the winner, 0 for everyone else.... just getting the feet wet & scoping the competition

Tour - 137.5 points for the winner, 62.5 points for second, 37.5 for third, and 12.5 points for fourth

Mid - 275 points for the winner, 125 points for second, 75 points for third, and 25 points for fourth

Major - 440 points for the winner, 200 points for second, 120 points for third, 40 points for fourth

Matches:

Match 1 (preseason) - 5/17/20 at American Classic Golf Club

Match 2 (tour) - 5/21/20 at Rookery South

Match 3 (tour) - 5/28/20 at Rookery North

Match 4 (mid) - 6/6/20 at Salt Pond

Match 5 (major) - 6/15/20 at Rehoboth CC
